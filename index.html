📚 Event Overview

SMCC-CCIS Week 2026 is a five-day campus celebration that highlights the creativity, innovation, and technical skills of students under the College of Computing and Information Sciences. Students will participate in coding competitions, technology exhibits, seminars, workshops, cybersecurity awareness activities, robotics demonstrations, and project presentations. The event encourages collaboration, leadership, creativity, and the responsible use of technology while providing opportunities for students to showcase their talents and learn from professionals in the IT industry.

🌟 Event Highlights

Featured Activities

  • 💻 Coding Hackathon for aspiring programmers.
  • 🤖 Robotics and Internet of Things (IoT) Exhibition.
  • 🛡️ Cybersecurity Awareness Seminar with guest speakers.
  • 📱 Mobile and Web Application Showcase.
  • 🎮 Tech Quiz Bowl and Interactive Games.
  • 🏆 Awarding Ceremony for outstanding participants.

Participants will have the opportunity to develop their technical skills, collaborate with fellow students, and gain valuable experience through hands-on activities and competitions.

📍 Event Location

The activities will be conducted at the Saint Michael College of Caraga, Atupan Street, Barangay 4, Nasipit, Agusan del Norte, Philippines.
